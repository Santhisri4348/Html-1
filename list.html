Train shall be designed to operate at a maximum service speed of 160 kmph. Train shall be designed so that train can operate safely at a maximum speed of 180 kmph during testing.

Train shall achieve a uniform full service braking across the whole speed range from 0 to 160 kmph.

The full service brake shall not achieve deceleration of greater than 1.0 m/s2 at any speed.

Train shall be capable of achieving a minimum an average acceleration of 0.7 m/s2 for speed up to 40 km/h, subject to the requirements with respect to jerk rate specified in Specification and Standards.

Train shall achieve a minimum deceleration of 0.8 m/s2 during full service braking following the jerk limit as specified.
